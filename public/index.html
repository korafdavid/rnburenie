CIF TRANSACTION
PROCEDURES

  1. Buyer confirms SCO and issues an official ICPO inserting seller's terms and procedures
  2. Seller issues FCO for buyer's signing, buyer returns signed FCO and a letter of procedures acceptance.
  3. Seller issues draft contract/SPA which is open for amendments. Buyer returns the signed draft contract along with NCNDA/IMFPA and a letter of guarantee to perform.
    • company license to export.
    • statement of availability of product.
    • commitment to supply the product.
    • certificate of origin.
    • analysis test result (product passport).
    ...
  4. Upon confirmation of the above POP document, Seller and buyer proceed and sign an Escrow Agreement with a mutually agreed Escrow company and the both parties proceed with the 5% payment to the Escrow account via MT103/ TT wire transfer, as guarantee that both parties will not default from the transaction.
  5. Seller bank issues the full set of proof of product (POP) and shipping documents via swift to buyer bank.
  6. Buyer within 7 banking days' issues DLC swift MT700 or SBLC swift MT760 according to Seller's fiduciary bank verbiage to seller nominated fiduciary offshore bank account for first month shipment.
  7. Seller issue 2%PB (Performance Bond) to activate buyer DLC/SBLC.
  8. Buyer pays for the total product value via TT/MT103 to the seller's bank after successful SGS/CIQ inspection at discharge port and tittle of product ownership transferred to buyer
  9. Seller pays all intermediaries involved in transaction as per signed NCNDA/IMFPA within 72 hours.

FROM TANK TO VESSEL/TANK PROCEDURE

  • Buyer issues ICPO upon acceptance of Seller's procedure CPA for Seller verification.
  • Seller issues the Commercial Invoice (CI) and Tank to Tank injection agreement (TTVIA) to be endorsed by buyer and its logistic Shipping Company
  • Upon received signed TTVIA from buyer and his logistics tank stronge company and signed Commercial Invoice (CI).
  • Seller legalizes the CI and provides Buyer with legalized documents and PPOP as listed below:
    • Product passport (Product analysis report) by SGS 72 hour
    • Injection Report.
    • Dip test authorization Letter (unconstitutional) and ATV.
    • Irrevocable commitment Letter to supply.
    • Export License.
    ...
  • Buyer within 24 hours after successful Dip Test on Seller's tanks provides Q88 for Seller to proceed for Tank to Vessel and provide Buyer with the Injection Report of the product into Buyer's Vessel.
  • Buyer makes payment for the total Cost of product injected into Buyer's vessel via MT 103, Seller transfers Tittle ownership to Buyer with all exportation documents required of the Buyer for the transaction.
  • seller will pay for all legalization from ministry and notary and pay for NCNDA to all Mandates after receive money from buyer in 24 hours.
  • Seller and Buyer sign one year contract upon conclusion of first lift transaction
